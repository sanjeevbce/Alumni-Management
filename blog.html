Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality, often abbreviated HPMOR, is a Harry Potter fan fiction by Eliezer
Yudkowsky. It adapts the story of Harry Potter by attempting to explain wizardry through the scientific method. It
was published as a serial from 28 February 2010 through to 14 March 2015.
